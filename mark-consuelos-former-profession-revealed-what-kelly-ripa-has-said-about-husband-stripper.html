Kelly Ripa's husband, Mark Consuelos', shocking former profession revealed

Publish date: 2024-07-02

The Live! host has been married to Mark for almost 30 years

    Kelly Ripa and Mark Consuelos have amassed an incredible $160 million fortune, but before they hit the big time, the actor struggled to make ends meet. 

    So much so, that the star had to take on a daring career as a stripper. Kelly previously spoke about her husband's decision and appeared to be more than on board with his daring profession.

    "So, there he was in South Florida, he's gorgeous, looking to break into show business, so he started off as a roadie to a group of these guys, and then they talked him into stripping."

    The mom-of-three even admitted that she keeps an old photo of Mark wearing a donkey thong from his dancing days.
