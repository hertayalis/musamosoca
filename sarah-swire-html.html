Sarah Swire - Trivia, Family, Bio

Publish date: 2024-05-23

Actress known for the CBC series Murdoch Mysteries. She played young Grace Mallory in the Amazon Prime series The Boys. She played Steph North in the 2018 film Anna and the Apocalypse. She also appeared in series including Frankie Drake Mysteries and Nurses.

Before Fame

She graduated from the Royal Conservatoire of Scotland in Glasgow. 

Trivia

Her first screen appearance was in the 2013 miniseries One Man Flash Mob: The Lucien Simon Story. She co-wrote the series with Lorn Macdonald. Her Hollywood debut was in the 2014 musical drama God Help the Girl. Throughout her career as an actress, she has made over a dozen appearances in different TV series' and films. She has also earned credit as a writer.

Family Life

She is from Canada. 

Associated With

Her character in The Boys was also played by Laila Robins.
