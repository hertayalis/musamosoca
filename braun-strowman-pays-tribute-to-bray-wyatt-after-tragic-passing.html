Braun Strowman Pays Tribute To Bray Wyatt After Tragic Passing

Publish date: 2024-08-19

Bray Wyatt made his WWE return during Extreme Rules last year and fans were elated at this shocking turn of events, as Wyatt was released by the company the prior year. Unfortunately, he could never return as Wyatt tragically passed away. Now it appears Braun Strowman paid tribute to Wyatt.

Bray Wyatt passed away at the age of 36, leaving behind a legacy and reputation that only a few could match. Many have now reacted to his tragic passing already.

Braun Strowman took to his social media and paid tribute to his Wyatt family brother, as he penned an emotional message and credited Wyatt for making him a better person.

i really don’t even know how or where to start. This is just what i can muster up right now.

8 years ago today I joined you as your black sheep. If I knew only an 8 short years later I would have to say good by I sure as hell wouldn’t have taken so much for granted. You were my best friend my mentor my big little brother my brother of destruction. You were there for all my highs and my lows as I was for yours. You taught me so many things in the business that we shared and loved and you taught me so much in life. You truly made me a better person. I was so honored the day I found out you were haveing Knash boy and you asked me to be his Godfather. Windham you were one of a kind with a great mind and even better soul. The world lost such and amazing man. My heart is with the Rotunda family JoJo and all the baby’s know i love you all so much. I’ll see you down the road my friend. Slap Brodie on the ass for me when you see him. I know this isn’t bye forever.

I LOVE YOU HOOT!!!

Bray Wyatt’s cause of death was revealed soon after the news of his passing broke. He suffered from a heart attack and passed away just when he was nearing his WWE return. Wyatt’s contributions to the industry will never be forgotten, as he remains in our hearts and minds always.
