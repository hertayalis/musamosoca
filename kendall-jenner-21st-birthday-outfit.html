Kendall Jenner 21st Birthday Party Outfits

Publish date: 2024-06-27

Kendall Jenner can finally have herself a cocktail—to fete that fact the model had a legendary yet low-key 21st birthday party in L.A. last night. Jenner made an entrance at a late night dinner at Catch in a lace-up semi sheer catsuit by British designer Julien Macdonald, finished with a green fur stole—styled by Monica Rose.

Getty Images

Kim Kardashian West made a rare appearance with Kanye West, as did sisters Kylie Jenner, Khloe and Kourtney Kardashian, Scott Disick, and both parents—as well as friend and fellow model Hailey Baldwin.

Continuing the festivities, the group went dancing at 1920s themed restaurant Delilah, according to People, where Jenner went method in a silver sequin mini dress— a custom LaBourjoisie creations covered in Swarovski crystals adding up to $9,000. "It's very lightweight and easy to move in," designer Antoine Salameh toldthe weekly. Also, according to Kendall, very old school Paris Hilton.
