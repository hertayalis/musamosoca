Triple Hs Booking Style to Take Over WWE Storylines Post-draft

Publish date: 2024-06-17

WWE fans are always looking for the inside scoop on what’s happening behind the scenes in their favorite wrestling company. Recently, Twitter insider account BoozerRasslin gave away some interesting information regarding the WWE Draft and the future direction of the company.

BoozerRasslin has been known for breaking WWE news and providing accurate information about draft picks and other WWE-related events. According to the account, the WWE Draft moves were part of the “first set of plays by Triple H” that are “set and done” with a few things still to work on until SummerSlam.

It seems that Triple H is taking a more prominent role in the company’s creative direction, and fans who enjoy his style of booking will be happy to see more of his fingerprints on storylines in the coming months.

One Twitter user asked if NXT talent would be able to finish up their storylines. BoozerRasslin replied that, as has been the case in recent years, this is expected to be the case. The account also tweeted that Triple H is “rounding up the last bits of the VKM era.”

As we move closer to SummerSlam, it will be interesting to see what other moves and changes Triple H has in store for WWE. Fans will undoubtedly be keeping a close eye on BoozerRasslin’s account for any new developments and inside information.
