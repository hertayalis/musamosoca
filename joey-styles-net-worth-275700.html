Joey Styles net worth: Joey Styles is an American professional wrestling commentator who has a net worth of $9 million. Joey Styles was born in The Bronx, New York City, new York in July 1971. He started his career with the North American Wrestling Alliance and became well known for his time with Extreme Championship Wrestling from 1993 to 2001.

He worked for the WWE from 2005 to 2008 before becoming their Vice President of Digital Media Content for WWE.com and the WWE app. He was released from the WWE in August 2016. Styles has also worked for the YES Network since 2012. Since being released from the WWE he is focused on becoming the voice of the indies and has worked for promotions including Beyond Wrestling, Chikara and Evolve, which he was terminated from for making a joke about announcer Joanna Rose and Donald Trump.
