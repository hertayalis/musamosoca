Tom Cruises marriage life: How many times has Tom Cruise been married?

Publish date: 2024-08-30

Tom Cruise, the American actor, and producer is famous for his numerous elite movies. He was exposed to the limelight for his major roles in the movies Risky Business (1983), and Top Gun (1986). He’s received numerous awards and bagged a number of grand nominations for his good works. Presently, he’s known for being one of the highest-paid actors worldwide. Tom has been in a union before and as well in a number of relationships. 

How many times did Tom Cruise marry?

The famous American actor has been married three times in his life which all ended sadly. First, Tom first married actress Mimi Rogers in 1987 whom he met after joining Scientology. Their marriage didn’t last the test of time as the two divorced in February 1990 and went their separate ways. He married for the second time to actress Nicole Kidman on December 24, 1990, whom he met on the set of the film Days of Thunder (1990). They got divorced in February 2001 at a time when the actress was unknowingly pregnant but unfortunately ended in a miscarriage. 

Eventually, he married again to Katie Holmes on November 18, 2006, after dating for a year.  They welcomed their daughter Suri in April of that year before their marriage. Their marriage hit the rocks and ended in 2012. 

What are the names of Tom Cruise’s ex-wives?

Tom’s first ex-wife is actress Mimi Rogers, whilst his second ex-wife’s name is actress Nicole Kidman. Actress Katie Holmes is the third woman to be the actor’s ex-wife. 

Is Tom Cruise still married?

As it stands, Cruise is not married since his last marriage with Katie Holmes ended in 2012. It is not known if he is in a relationship but has decided to keep it out of the public eye or he’s single for now.  

What is Tom Cruise’s longest marriage?

His third and last marriage with the Dawson’s Creek star Katie Holmes is the longest of all his marriages. The two movie stars were in a union for six years, that’s from 2016 to 2012.
