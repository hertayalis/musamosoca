Net Worth and Salary

Lou Diamond Phillips is a multi-talented American actor, writer, director, and producer who has made a name for himself in the entertainment industry. Early Life and Education

Lou Diamond Phillips was born on February 17, 1962, in Subic Bay Naval Station, Philippines. His father was an American of Scotch-Irish descent while his mother was a Filipina. Phillips’ parents met during the Vietnam War where his father was stationed as a naval officer. His family moved frequently during his early life due to his father’s position in the military. They later settled in Texas, where Phillips attended Flour Bluff High School and graduated in 1980.

After high school, Phillips attended the University of Texas at Arlington, initially studying drama but later switched to music. He dropped out of college in his senior year to pursue his acting career.

Career

Phillips’ acting career began in 1984 when he landed his breakthrough role as Ritchie Valens in the movie La Bamba. His exceptional performance earned him a Golden Globe nomination for Best Supporting Actor. Phillips continued to earn critical acclaim for his performance in his subsequent movies such as Stand and Deliver, Young Guns, and Courage Under Fire.

Apart from his work in movies, Phillips has worked in various television series such as Longmire, the Law and Order franchise, and the Broadway production of The King and I. He has also worked as a director and writer in films like American Me and the TV movie Love Takes Wing.

Personal Life

Phillips has been married three times. He first married Julie Cypher, a producer and director, in 1987 but they divorced in 1990. He later married Kelly Phillips, an artist, in 1994. The couple had three children, but they separated in 2004 and divorced in 2007. Philips married Yvonne Boismier, a makeup artist, in 2007, and they have one daughter together.

In 2017, Phillips was arrested for a DWI (Driving While Intoxicated) in Portland, Texas, but he later apologized for his actions and successfully completed a court-ordered alcohol education program.

Real Estate

Phillips owns a home in Sherman Oaks, California, that he purchased for $821,000 in 2013. The property has five bedrooms, four bathrooms, a swimming pool, and a spa. The estimated value of the property is now over $1.4 million.

Legacy
