Get ready for Nampa's 2023 Festival of the Arts: A weekend packed with creativity and family fun

Publish date: 2024-05-26

NAMPA, Idaho — The 2023 Nampa Festival of the Arts is just around the corner. Saturday, August 12, from 9 a.m. to 6 p.m. and on Sunday August 13, from 10 a.m. to 3 p.m.

The Festival will be held at Lakeview Park and this year will feature over 200 art and craft exhibitors. Food and entertainment both days, including local entertainment on the TDS Fiber Main Stage. There will be free kids activities, so bring the whole family down.

The Festival will also have a juried fine art show, so make plans to swing by the fine arts section of the festival as well. For more information, click here.
