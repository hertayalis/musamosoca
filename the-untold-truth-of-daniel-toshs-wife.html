The Untold Truth Of Daniel Tosh's Wife

Publish date: 2024-07-20

Behind every good television show is a good team of writers. Luckily for Daniel Tosh, he had Carly Hallam on his team. In August 2020, it was announced that Tosh.0 would be ending after its 12th season. Hallam was around for quite a few of those seasons as she joined Tosh.0 as a writer in 2012, according to People. Prior to joining the writing team, Hallam appeared in several episodes of the viral clip show. And Hallam's not only credited for her work on Tosh.0, she also has writing credits on NBC's Brooklyn Nine-Nine.

In addition to writing for television, Hallam also has published work on the internet. Her article about how to wear a little black dress appears on a notorious satire site called McSweeney's.

Hallam and Tosh seem to share a similar sense of sarcastic humor. While her Instagram page is private, Hallam's Twitter page is public and exhibits some of her humorous commentary. In her pinned tweet, she writes: "Would you like a printed receipt or would you like us to email you 3-5 times a week for the rest of your life?" It seems that Tosh has met his match made in comedy heaven. How romantic!
