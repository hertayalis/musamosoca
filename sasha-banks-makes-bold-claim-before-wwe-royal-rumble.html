Sasha Banks Makes Bold Claim Before WWE Royal Rumble

Publish date: 2024-07-14

Sasha Banks remains one of the most decorated and talented female WWE Superstars over the last few years. She has taken part in numerous classic matches over the years and fans can always count on her for a great performance. After a surprises return before the Royal Rumble, The Boss has a hot take to share.

Sasha Banks was supposed to be out of action for 6 to 8 weeks after she injured her leg while wrestling Charlotte Flair at a recent live event. It was expected she would miss out on the Women’s Royal Rumble match.

The former Smackdown Women’s Champion had other plans as she returned on this week’s episode of Friday Night Smackdown. After her triumphant return, Sasha Banks took to Twitter and claimed that she will win the Women’s Royal Rumble and main event WrestleMania 38.

I am going to win #RoyalRumble and go on to Main Event #WrestleMania again!

I’m getting what I want

We will have to wait and see whether she will end up winning the Women’s Royal Rumble match this year or not as Banks certainly has a lot of momentum now that she is back. We will have complete coverage here at Ringside News!
